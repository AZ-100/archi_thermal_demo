Code of Conduct

Welcome to ArchiThermal! We are committed to maintaining a positive and respectful environment for all users. By using our website, you agree to abide by the following Code of Conduct.

1. Respect and Integrity

We expect all users to treat each other with respect and dignity. Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech will not be tolerated.

2. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for providing accurate information and respecting the privacy of others. All users must comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Prohibited Conduct

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

  • Spamming or posting irrelevant content
  • Engaging in illegal activities
  • Posting offensive or harmful content

4. Intellectual Property

Users must respect the intellectual property rights of others. Unauthorized use of copyrighted materials is not permitted.

5. Content Moderation

We reserve the right to monitor and review content posted by users. Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in content removal or account suspension.

6. User-generated Content

Users are responsible for any content they post. All content must comply with our standards and be free of offensive material.

7. Privacy and Data Protection

Please review our Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

8. Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in consequences, including account suspension or termination.

9. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Code of Conduct, please contact us at:

Email: info@archithermal.com.au
